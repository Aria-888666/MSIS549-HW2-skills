## Executive Decision Memo Writer

### Purpose

Translate structured analysis into a professional, executive-ready recommendation memo.

---

### When to Use

Use after:
- Evaluation and ranking are complete.

Do NOT use when:
- The analysis phase is incomplete.
- Major clarification questions remain unresolved.

---

### Inputs

- Evaluation output from the Decision Evaluation Engine

---

### Outputs

Produce a memo including:

- **Executive Summary**
- **Context**
- **Alternatives Considered**
- **Analytical Summary**
- **Recommendation**
- **Risks & Sensitivities**
- **Next Steps**


---

### Step-by-Step Behavior

1. Summarize the core decision clearly.
2. Present alternatives concisely.
3. Explain evaluation logic.
4. Provide a clear recommendation.
5. Explicitly state risks.
6. Identify conditions under which recommendation would change.
7. Provide actionable next steps.

---

### Constraints

- No fluff.
- No fabricated numbers.
- Clear and concise language.
- Explicit uncertainty acknowledgment.
- Professional, executive tone.

---

### Failure Modes

- Generic executive language without analytical substance.
- Recommendation without rationale.
- Ignoring sensitivity analysis.
- Overconfidence in uncertain outcomes.
